About Us

Philly Born. Comfort Obsessed.

Ice Peak Heating & Cooling started with one truck, one toolbox, and a simple promise: show up on time, quote an honest price, and do the job right the first time. Fifteen years later, that promise hasn't changed — only our fleet has grown.

We're a local, family-owned company serving homeowners across Greater Philadelphia. Every technician is background-checked, factory-trained, and empowered to treat your home like their own — shoe covers and all.
